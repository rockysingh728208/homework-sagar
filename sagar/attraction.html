Welcome to Dubrovnik, a city steeped in history, adorned with architectural
wonders, and surrounded by natural beauty. Explore the captivating
attractions that make Dubrovnik and its surroundings a destination like
no other.

Dubrovnik and its surroundings are a treasure trove of attractions, each telling a
story of the city’s rich history and natural splendor. Whether you’re drawn to
historical landmarks, panoramic views, or cultural experiences, Dubrovnik has
something to captivate every visitor. Explore these attractions to make the
most of your journey through this Adriatic gem.

Old Town

Step back in time as you wander through the cobbled streets of Dubrovnik’s Old
Town. Enclosed by magnificent city walls, the Old Town is a living museum,
boasting Gothic, Renaissance, and Baroque architecture. Highlights include the
iconic Pile Gate, the Rector’s Palace, and the vibrant Stradun, the main street.

About this UNESCO World Heritage Site →

City Walls

Ascend the ancient city walls for a panoramic view of Dubrovnik’s rooftops and
the azure Adriatic Sea. The walls, offering a glimpse into the city’s military past,
provide a unique perspective of the Old Town and its surroundings.

About the City Walls →

Fort Lovrijenac

Perched on a rocky outcrop, Fort Lovrijenac offers not only historical
significance but also breathtaking views of the Adriatic. Explore the
fortress and revel in its role in Dubrovnik’s past while enjoying a stunning
sunset backdrop.

About Fort Lovrijenac →

Banje Beach

Relax on the shores of Banje Beach, a pristine stretch of sand just steps away
from the Old Town. The beach offers a perfect blend of sun and sea, providing a
tranquil escape with views of the city walls and the open sea.

About Banje Beach →

Lokrum Island

Venture to Lokrum Island, a botanical paradise boasting lush gardens and
historical landmarks. Explore the ruins, relax by the Dead Sea, and revel in the
island’s serene ambiance.

About Lokrum Island →

Mount Srđ

Ascend Mount Srđ for a panoramic view of Dubrovnik and the Adriatic. Whether
reached by cable car or hiking, the summit offers a spectacular vantage point
for capturing the cityscape and the surrounding landscapes.

About the Mount Srđ →

Dubrovnik Cathedral

Discover the beauty of Dubrovnik Cathedral, a Baroque masterpiece housing
relics and artwork. Admire the cathedral’s architecture and explore its interior,
including the Treasury with its valuable artifacts.

About the Dubrovnik Cathedral →

Dubrovnik
Summer Festival

Immerse yourself in the cultural extravaganza of the Dubrovnik Summer
Festival. The Old Town transforms into a stage for music, theater, and dance,
providing a unique opportunity to experience the arts against the backdrop of
historical landmarks.

About the Dubrovnik Summer Festival →

Lokrum Game
of Thrones Tour

For fans of the iconic TV series, embark on a Game of Thrones tour of Lokrum
Island. Visit filming locations, including the Iron Throne, and relive moments
from the show in the very settings where they were filmed.

About the Lokrum Game of Thrones Tour →

Elafiti Islands

Escape the city and explore the Elafiti Islands, including Koločep, Šipan, and
Lopud. Each island offers a unique charm, from pristine beaches and charming
villages to historical sites and untouched landscapes.

About Elafiti Islands →
